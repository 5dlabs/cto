# Questions to Ask in a DevOps Interview (AWS Environment)

## Team & Culture

1. **How is the DevOps/Platform team structured? Is it centralized or embedded within product teams?**
   - Helps understand if you'll be a service provider or embedded partner

2. **What does on-call look like? What's the rotation frequency and typical incident volume?**
   - Critical for work-life balance expectations

3. **How do you handle incidents? Is there a blameless postmortem culture?**
   - Reveals psychological safety and learning orientation

4. **What's the ratio of proactive work (new tooling, improvements) vs reactive work (tickets, firefighting)?**
   - Indicates team maturity and technical debt levels

5. **How do developers and DevOps collaborate? Who's responsible for application monitoring and deployment?**
   - Shows where ownership boundaries lie

## Technical Environment

6. **What does your current AWS architecture look like at a high level? Multi-account? What services are you most invested in?**
   - Understand the landscape you'd be working in

7. **What IaC tools do you use? Terraform, CloudFormation, CDK? How mature is the codebase?**
   - Gauge technical debt and modernization state

8. **How do deployments work today? What does the CI/CD pipeline look like?**
   - Understand automation maturity

9. **Are you running containers? ECS, EKS, or something else? What led to that choice?**
   - Shows decision-making process and current investment

10. **What's your monitoring and observability stack? CloudWatch, Datadog, Grafana, something else?**
    - Important for day-to-day work experience

11. **How do you manage secrets and sensitive configuration?**
    - Security maturity indicator

## Challenges & Priorities

12. **What's the biggest infrastructure challenge you're facing right now?**
    - Honest answer reveals what you'd actually be working on

13. **What would success look like for this role in the first 90 days? First year?**
    - Clarifies expectations and priorities

14. **Is there any legacy infrastructure you're trying to migrate away from? What's the timeline?**
    - Understand potential tedious work vs greenfield opportunities

15. **How do you balance security/compliance requirements with developer velocity?**
    - Shows how they handle competing priorities

## Growth & Development

16. **What does career progression look like for someone in this role?**
    - IC track vs management, title levels

17. **Is there budget for training, certifications, or conferences?**
    - Investment in professional development

18. **How do you stay current with AWS releases and industry practices?**
    - Learning culture indicator

## Process & Workflow

19. **How do changes get reviewed and approved? What's the PR process like for infrastructure code?**
    - Understand bureaucracy vs autonomy balance

20. **What's the release cadence? How often does code get deployed to production?**
    - Continuous deployment vs scheduled releases

21. **How do you handle cost management and optimization in AWS?**
    - Shows operational maturity

## Red Flag Detection Questions

22. **What happened to the last person in this role?** (if backfill)
    - Listen for warning signs

23. **What's something you'd change about how the team operates if you could?**
    - Honest interviewers reveal real challenges

24. **How has the team's scope or size changed in the past year?**
    - Growth trajectory or contraction signals

---

## Tips for Asking These Questions

- **Prioritize**: Pick 5-7 questions most important to you; you won't have time for all
- **Adapt**: Ask different questions to different interviewers (hiring manager vs peer vs skip-level)
- **Listen for specifics**: Vague answers like "we're working on improving that" are yellow flags
- **Take notes**: Write down answers to compare across companies
