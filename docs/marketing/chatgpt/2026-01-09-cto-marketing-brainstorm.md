# CTO Platform - Marketing Brainstorm (ChatGPT)

## Taglines (3–5) + rationale

1. **A whole engineering team. Minus the cloud tax.**  
   Clear “team, not tool” + cost angle in plain language; feels modern and slightly edgy.

2. **Describe it. Review the PR. Ship.**  
   Emphasizes the workflow (GitHub → PRs) and “managed everything” without buzzwords.

3. **Your repo gets a staff.**  
   Short, memorable, and communicates multi-agent specialization better than “assistant.”

4. **Infrastructure included. Engineers included.**  
   Punchy and explicit; reads like a product label, not hype.

5. **From issue to merge—handled.**  
   Outcome-forward; implies orchestration across roles (PM → impl → QA → integration).

## Category suggestions (1–2)

1. **Engineering Team Platform (ETP)**  
   A “team” category, not a tool category. Positions CTO above copilots and below outsourcing.

2. **Managed Agent DevOps**  
   Signals orchestration + CI/CD + infra, but stays grounded in developer reality.

## Buzzwords / coined terms (become “our thing”)

- **Cloud tax**: The premium you pay for convenience; CTO “refunds” it with bare metal.
- **PR-first development**: Everything becomes a reviewable pull request, end-to-end.
- **Multi-agent staff**: A staff of specialists, not a generalist chat.
- **Opsless shipping**: Ship without running Kubernetes or babysitting infra.
- **Bare-metal runway**: More product months per dollar because compute isn’t eating your budget.
- **Orchestrated engineering**: The system coordinates roles like a real team (PM, impl, QA, integration).

## Hero section copy (headline + subheadline)

### Option A (team + cost)
**A full engineering team that runs on bare metal.**  
Connect your Git repo (GitHub, GitLab, or Gitea), describe what you want, and review PRs from specialists—backend, frontend, QA, integration, and more. Infrastructure, CI/CD, and monitoring included. No cloud tax.

### Option B (workflow + managed everything)
**Describe the work. We deliver the PR.**  
CTO coordinates a team of specialists and the infrastructure they need—so you can ship without hiring, DevOps, or surprise cloud bills.

### Option C (category framing)
**Engineering Team Platform—built for shipping.**  
Not a copilot. Not a chatbot. A coordinated staff of specialists that turns issues into reviewed, test-backed PRs on managed bare metal.
