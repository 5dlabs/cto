## 🪙 CRYPTO & DEFI

### 5. DeFAI: Autonomous DeFi Agents

**Core Definition:** AI agents as autonomous programs that think, decide, and act on DeFi tasks:
- Trading, yield farming
- Lending optimization
- Arbitrage
- Risk management
- Portfolio rebalancing

**Critical Infrastructure Requirements:**

1. **Seamless Interoperability** - Agents must move across chains (Ethereum, Solana, Base, Avalanche, BNB)
   - Problem: Bridging adds delays, costs, failure risk
   - Solution needed: Atomic cross-chain execution

2. **Scale & Congestion** - When thousands of agents act simultaneously, network becomes bottleneck

**Platform Relevance:** CRITICAL - This is exactly what we're building
