## Channel Strategy (Prioritized)

### Tier 1: Free, Immediate Impact

**1. X/Twitter — Build in Public (Daily)**

The #1 channel for reaching blockchain founders. Crypto Twitter is where deals start.

- Post daily from 5D Labs: agent screenshots, infra dashboards, cost comparisons, Solana trading progress
- Thread format for depth: "Here's how 13 AI agents shipped a feature today" with screenshots
- Engage with Solana, AI agent, and DevOps communities
- Use `xurl` (already in the runtime image) for API-powered posting and monitoring
- **Target hashtags**: #BuildInPublic #Solana #AIAgents #DevOps #Kubernetes #OpenClaw
- **Time investment**: 30 min/day
- **Expected first lead**: 4-8 weeks

**2. Telegram — Crypto Community Presence (Weekly)**

Telegram (800M+ users) is where crypto-native founders make decisions and evaluate vendors.

- Join: Solana developer channels, DeFi builder groups, infra discussion groups
- Mine incubator portfolios (Alliance DAO, Animoca Brands, Outlier Ventures) for projects before they go public
- Watch for projects preparing token launches — they have 2x higher reply rates for infra services
- Answer questions, share expertise, build reputation — not salesy
- **Time investment**: 2-3 hrs/week
- **Expected first lead**: 2-6 weeks

**3. Discord — Technical Community (Ongoing)**

- Developer DAO, Solana Discord, Kubernetes Slack, CNCF channels
- Already building in these ecosystems — be helpful, share real solutions
- **Time investment**: Passive, integrated with daily work

### Tier 2: Low Cost, Medium-Term

**4. Content Marketing — Pillar Articles (Weekly)**

Three pillar articles that each target a segment:

| Article | Target Segment | Publish On |
|---|---|---|
| "How We Cut Cloud Costs 70% With Bare Metal" | Modernization teams | Dev.to, HN |
| "Building Trading Agents on Solana With AI" | Blockchain founders | Dev.to, Solana forums |
| "13 AI Agents Ship Production Code — Here's How" | Early-stage startups | Dev.to, Medium |

Cross-post excerpts on X/Twitter. Dev.to is free and has strong SEO.

- **Time investment**: 1 article every 1-2 weeks
- **Expected first lead**: 6-12 weeks (compounds over time)

**5. Farcaster — Early Mover (When Time Permits)**

Decentralized social protocol gaining traction among Web3 thought leaders. Early mover advantage for positioning before it gets crowded.

### Tier 3: Paid, After Traction Established

**6. LinkedIn — Organic First, Paid Later**

LinkedIn is complementary, not primary, for blockchain clients. Use it for:

- Professional credibility (updated profile, recommendations)
- 2 organic posts/week (repurpose X content)
- The posted LinkedIn blurb to announce consulting availability
- **Do NOT buy Premium/Sales Navigator yet** — save $1,200/year until inbound is flowing

When to upgrade: Once receiving 2-3 inbound inquiries/month from Tier 1-2 channels, consider Sales Navigator Core ($100/mo) for targeted outreach to the non-crypto modernization segment specifically.

**7. Consulting Marketplaces (When Time Allows)**

- Toptal — premium positioning for $225/hr advisory tier
- Clarity.fm — paid advisory calls
- Arc.dev — remote-focused, popular with US startups
- Clutch.co / GoodFirms — B2B service directories

**8. Podcast Guest Appearances (When Time Allows)**

One good appearance generates months of inbound. Target:

- Crypto infrastructure podcasts
- AI/automation podcasts
- DevOps/SRE podcasts
- **Not a priority right now** given time constraints, but keep a list of targets

## LinkedIn Decision

**Current recommendation: Skip Premium/Sales Navigator.**

| Factor | Assessment |
|---|---|
| Cost | $100-180/month ($1,200-2,160/year) |
| InMail response rate | 10-15% (and lower for crypto) |
| Blockchain founder activity | Low — they're on Telegram/X |
| Enterprise modernization | Better fit, but secondary segment |
| API capabilities | Locked down — no automation allowed |
| Account risk | Third-party automation tools risk bans |
| ROI for solo consultant | Questionable vs enterprise sales teams |

**Revisit in 60-90 days** once organic channels are producing leads.
