## 3. Information we collect

We aim to collect **minimal** information.

### 3.1 Information you do not send to our servers for exports

When you generate PDF or PowerPoint files, that processing happens **in your browser** (client-side). We do **not** need an account with us for those features, and we do **not** receive the deck content on our servers solely because you exported a file.

### 3.2 Optional “Save to Google Drive”

If you use **Save to Google Drive**, Google’s sign-in flow runs in your browser. We request permission to create files in **your** Google Drive using the restricted **Google Drive `drive.file`** scope, which is intended to allow access **only to files created or opened by this application** (not your entire Drive).

We **do not** host a login system for Google on our backend for this static site: authentication is between **your browser** and **Google**. We do **not** store your Google password.

### 3.3 Technical and operational data

Like most websites, our hosting provider and infrastructure may process **technical data** automatically, such as:

- IP address, user agent, and request metadata  
- approximate location derived from IP (typically at a regional level)  
- security and abuse-prevention signals  

We use this information to **operate, secure, and improve** the Service and to understand aggregate usage.

### 3.4 Communications

If you contact us (for example by email), we receive whatever information **you choose** to include in that message.
